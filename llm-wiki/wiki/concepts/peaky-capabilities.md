# Peaky Capabilities

The source describes current AI progress as "peaky": dramatic improvements are concentrated in a relatively narrow set of technical domains rather than distributed evenly across all common user tasks [Karpathy x: AI Capability Perception Gap](../sources/karpathy-x.md). `(high confidence)`

## Domains Highlighted As Strong

- Programming
- Math
- Research

## Domains Highlighted As Less Dramatically Improved

- Search
- Writing
- Advice

## Explanations Offered

- Some technical domains offer clearer training signals, which links to [[verifiable-rewards]]
- Some technical domains matter more commercially, so companies prioritize them more heavily

## Implication

A single headline about "AI capability" can be misleading because performance may be rising rapidly in one slice of the landscape while remaining unimpressive in another. This concept is central to [[ai-capability-perception-gap]].
